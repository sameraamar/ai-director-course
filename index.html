    Welcome to AI Director Course

    Build a polished 30-to-60-second cinematic AI video in seven focused days.

    What You Will Make

    By the end of this sprint, you should have: - A storyboarded concept - A visually consistent set of generated frames - Motion-enhanced clips - Optional dialogue or voiceover - Music and sound design - A final edited video ready for sharing, pitching, or portfolio use

    How the Sprint Works

    Every day uses a two-speed format: - Fast Track: the shortest path to making progress today - Deep Dive: theory, troubleshooting, and craft notes for stronger results

    Sprint Map

    1. Day 1: Break the idea into a precise 5-shot plan
    2. Day 2: Turn the plan into strong keyframes
    3. Day 3: Add believable motion
    4. Day 4: Generate voice and lip sync when needed
    5. Day 5: Build mood with music and sound effects
    6. Day 6: Edit everything into one cohesive piece
    7. Day 7: Review, polish, upscale, and export

    Before You Start

    • Read the Tool Vault for current tool categories.
    • Create a project folder for your assets.
    • Decide whether your final piece is an ad, product teaser, or portfolio short.

    Suggested Outcome Standard

    Aim for one final video that is: - Short enough to finish this week - Strong enough to publish - Clear enough that someone else understands the story without explanation
